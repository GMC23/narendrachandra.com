About Narendra Chandra

Music with a cinematic soul

Narendra Chandra is an Indian music director, composer, singer and lyricist associated with Bollywood and independent music. His work spans film soundtrack contributions, romantic originals, devotional releases, pop, rock and EDM-inspired productions.

His professional portfolio includes a music and lyrics credit connected with the Yash Raj Films production Sandeep Aur Pinky Faraar, alongside a growing catalogue of independently released music and videos.

Sandeep Aur Pinky Faraar

A Yash Raj Films production directed by Dibakar Banerjee. Narendra Chandra is presented in this portfolio as a music composer and lyricist associated with the soundtrack, including the songs “Don” and “iPhone”.
